Times Square

Marriott Marquis Hotel

5 PLY

We have all been there- walking around NYC and having the sudden urge to pee with no idea where to go. Some places in the city can be harder than others to find a clean accessible bathroom. One of these places is Times Square. Well, here is one hidden gem you must jot down--the Marriott Marquis hotel. You can walk past concierge and take the elevator up to their eighth floor public bathroom.
